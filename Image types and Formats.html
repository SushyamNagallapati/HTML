In computer we have 2 types of images, they are; 1. Raster Images 2. Vector Images ### 1. Raster Images --> Raster images are made up of pixels. --> They come from cameras/ scanners. --> Formats: JPG (Mostly used), Gif and PNG (Used for animations and tranparency), WEBP. --> More pixels = larger file size. --> Images look blurry when enlarged. ### 2. Vector Images --> Created in software like Adobe Illustrator. --> Formats: SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics). --> Images look sharp at any size, because images are made up of mathematical vectors and not by pixels.
